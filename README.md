# BuddyOS: AI-Powered Life Operating System

BuddyOS is a premium, next-generation AI operating system designed to elevate productivity for students and professionals. Moving away from traditional static planners, it acts as a dynamic cognitive assistant featuring predictive analytics, digital twins, and personalized long-term guidance.

##  Architecture & Visual Style
* **Theme:** Soft Pastel AI Aesthetic with Glassmorphism and Smooth Animations.
* **Colors:** Lavender, Baby Blue ,Soft Pink , Mint Green, Cream White
* **Tech Stack:** Flutter (Frontend), Node.js (Backend), Python (AI Forecasting Models), MongoDB/PostgreSQL (Database).

## Key Features

### Student Mode (AI Academic Life OS)
* **Future Self AI:** An interactive avatar simulating future career paths (e.g., Google Engineer) offering personalized milestone tracking.
* **Opportunity Intelligence Network:** Centralized discovery hub recommending hackathons and scholarships with an AI Match Percentage.
* **Student Digital Twin:** Analyzes study habits to forecast success scores, burnout risks, and deadline alerts.
* **AI Decision Copilot:** Evaluates choices (e.g., "GSoC vs Internship") and provides risk analysis.

### Professional Mode (Executive OS)
* **Professional Digital Twin:** Tracks work patterns to measure career growth and promotion readiness.
* **Meeting Intelligence System:** Automatically extracts actionable items and deadlines from notes, images, or audio.
* **Networking Copilot:** Manages professional relationships with custom follow-up recommendations.
